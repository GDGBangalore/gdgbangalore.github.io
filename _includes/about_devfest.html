DevFest

GDG DevFests are large, community-run events that offers speaker sessions across multiple product areas, all-day hack-a-thons, code labs, and much more. Each GDG DevFest is inspired by and uniquely tailored to the needs of the local developer community.

Each GDG DevFest will, at its core, be powered by a shared belief that when developers come together to exchange ideas, amazing things can happen.
